No 1 About Project

An endless shooter game where the aliens will be spawned based on the wave number.
The whole project is done in Unreal Blueprints using Unreal Engine 4.27.

Enemies(spiders) spawn at various points and waves increase one by one. Health pickups are available. The spider will follow you only in the zone and if you get out of the zone you will receive a warning and if you stay there you will instantly die. The controls are WASD and mouse for direction and also shooting direction. There is a health bar and also a kill count meter. The sound effects are added at the Notify event of the animation.

No 2 The Process

The game is an infinte shooter game where the infinte part is that there are n number of waves. The project was done as a part of the course in udemy. I have used only Unreal Blueprints.

No 2.2Game Design

  • An out of bounds volume is created to make the player face the enemies within a specific area. When the player goes out of the volume a warning will be displayed and the player will face imminent death in 5sec.
  • GameMode->BP_GameMode
    Wave of difficulty is created and based on the wave the enemies count increases. This is done in the BP_GameMode.
    The next wave will begin in 2sec after the end of each wave.,
